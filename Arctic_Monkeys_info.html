Arctic Monkeys

The Car

Far harder to parse than its immediate predecessor, 2018's Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino, The Car is, nonetheless, an achingly bleak album of leave-takings; opaquely beautiful, like a classic car with tinted windows. It's very much a farewell to LA, where this Sheffield band have spent much of the past decade.
