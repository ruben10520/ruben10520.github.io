Vinyl Moon

050

Vinyl Moon's "050" is an eclectic and immersive compilation that showcases the label's commitment to curating unique and diverse collections of music. The album features a diverse range of artists and genres, including indie rock, electronic, and experimental music. From the dreamy melodies of Karmic's "Wanted to Love You" to the haunting vocals of Salt Cathedral's "Go and Get It," "050" offers a fascinating journey through different moods and sounds. The album's standout tracks demonstrate Vinyl Moon's talent for discovering promising emerging artists and creating captivating listening experiences. Overall, "050" is a must-listen for anyone seeking fresh and innovative music.
